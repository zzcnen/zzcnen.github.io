四、重点句型

  1. where引导定语从句
    The small restaurant where we tasted local snacks has been open for over 20 years.
    我们品尝当地小吃的那家小餐馆已经营业20多年了。
  2. as引导非限制性定语从句
    As is known to all, the Spring Festival is the most important traditional festival for Chinese people.
    众所周知，春节是中国人最重要的传统节日。
  3. it作形式宾语（find+it+adj.+to do sth.）
    Many athletes find it challenging to balance training and daily life.
    许多运动员发现平衡训练和日常生活很有挑战性。
  4. while引导对比状语从句
    While some people prefer sweet food, others are fond of savory dishes.
    有些人喜欢甜食，而另一些人则偏爱咸味菜肴。

五、单元写作
Unit 1 Food for Thought
一、单元介绍
本单元围绕“饮食”这一核心话题展开，涵盖饮食文化差异、传统美食与现代饮食观念、健康饮食等内容。单元内应用文写作侧重饮食相关的介绍文、建议信（如推荐特色美食、给出健康饮食建议）、邀请信（如邀请他人品尝家乡美食）等，旨在引导学生运用恰当的语言描述饮食特点、传递饮食文化内涵，或针对饮食问题提出合理建议。
二、通用表达
① 话题单词
饮食相关：cuisine（菜系）、delicacy（美食）、snack（小吃）、staple food（主食）、dessert（甜点）、appetizer（开胃菜）、ingredient（食材）、spice（调料）、recipe（食谱）
烹饪相关：fry（油炸）、steam（蒸）、boil（煮）、braise（炖）、roast（烤）、cook（烹饪）、prepare（准备）、flavor（调味）
饮食观念与健康：balanced diet（均衡饮食）、nutritious（有营养的）、healthy（健康的）、junk food（垃圾食品）、calorie（卡路里）、digest（消化）、nutrition（营养）
文化相关：custom（习俗）、tradition（传统）、cultural difference（文化差异）、local（当地的）、regional（地区性的）
② 段首句、主题句、段尾句
段首句：
a)When it comes to food, different countries have their own unique cuisines and eating customs.
b)My hometown is famous for a variety of delicious snacks that are deeply loved by locals and tourists alike.
c)Maintaining a balanced diet is essential for our physical health and daily work and study.
d)I would like to recommend a special dish to you, which is a classic representative of our local cuisine.
主题句：
a)The biggest difference between Eastern and Western cuisines lies in the cooking methods and the concept of food matching.
b)This local snack is not only tasty but also carries the rich cultural memory of our hometown.
c)To keep healthy, we should avoid junk food and intake more fresh vegetables and fruits.
d)The recipe of this dish is simple, but it requires great attention to the heat and the proportion of ingredients.
段尾句：
a)In short, exploring different food cultures can help us better understand the diversity of the world.

b)If you have the chance to visit my hometown, don't miss the opportunity to taste this delicious snack.
c)Only by developing good eating habits can we enjoy a healthy and happy life.
d)I believe you will fall in love with this dish as soon as you taste it.
Unit 2 Let’s Celebrate!
一、单元介绍
本单元聚焦“节日庆典”主题，涉及中外传统节日（如春节、中秋节、圣诞节、感恩节）、节日的起源与习俗、庆典活动的组织与参与等内容。应用文写作主要包括节日介绍文、活动邀请信、节日祝福信、活动策划书（简要）等，要求学生能够清晰阐述节日的背景、特色习俗，准确传达节日祝福，或合理规划节日相关活动。
二、通用表达
① 话题单词
节日名称：Spring Festival（春节）、Mid-Autumn Festival（中秋节）、Christmas（圣诞节）、Thanksgiving Day（感恩节）、Dragon Boat Festival（端午节）、Lantern Festival（元宵节）
节日习俗：decorate（装饰）、gather（聚集）、celebrate（庆祝）、exchange gifts（交换礼物）、have a feast（设宴）、set off fireworks（放烟花）、admire the moon（赏月）、eat dumplings/zongzi/mooncakes（吃饺子/粽子/月饼）
庆典相关：celebration（庆典）、activity（活动）、ceremony（仪式）、parade（游行）、performance（表演）、blessing（祝福）、joy（喜悦）、tradition（传统）
情感与动作：happy（快乐的）、joyful（喜悦的）、excited（兴奋的）、invite（邀请）、attend（参加）、prepare（准备）、share（分享）
② 段首句、主题句、段尾句
段首句：
a)Festivals are an important part of every culture, carrying people's expectations for a better life.
b)The Spring Festival is the most important traditional festival in China, with a long history and rich customs.
c)To celebrate Thanksgiving Day, our class plans to hold a special activity and I'd like to invite you to join us.
d)Different countries have different festivals, but the desire for happiness and harmony behind them is the same.
主题句：
a)The customs of the Mid-Autumn Festival, such as admiring the moon and eating mooncakes, are closely related to the theme of family reunion.
b)The core of this festival activity is to let everyone experience the charm of traditional culture and enhance mutual feelings.
c)Christmas is not only a religious festival but also a time for people to gather with family and friends and share joys.
d)The origin of the Dragon Boat Festival is closely linked to the patriotic poet Qu Yuan, and it has been passed down for thousands of years.
段尾句：
a)I hope you can experience the strong festive atmosphere with us and enjoy the happiness of the festival.
b)These traditional festival customs are precious cultural heritage that we should inherit and carry forward.
c)May this festival bring you peace, joy and all the best wishes.
d)Celebrating festivals together is a good way to strengthen the bond between people and promote cultural communication.
Unit 3 On the Move
一、单元介绍
本单元以“运动”为核心主题，内容涵盖各类运动项目、运动的益处、运动与健康的关系、运动赛事与运动精神、运动安全等。应用文写作主要包括运动介绍文、运动建议信（如推荐适合的运动项目、给出运动安全提示）、运动赛事通知、加入运动俱乐部的申请信等，旨在让学生能够清晰介绍运动项目的特点，合理提出运动相关建议，或规范撰写运动相关的实用文书。
二、通用表达
① 话题单词
运动项目：basketball（篮球）、football（足球）、swimming（游泳）、running（跑步）、cycling（骑行）、badminton（羽毛球）、tennis（网球）、gymnastics（体操）、marathon（马拉松）
运动益处与健康：strengthen（增强）、fit（健康的）、physical fitness（体能）、relax（放松）、relieve stress（缓解压力）、improve（提高）、endurance（耐力）、flexibility（灵活性）
运动相关动作与装备：play（进行运动）、practice（练习）、compete（竞争）、win（赢得）、lose（输掉）、sportswear（运动服）、sports shoes（运动鞋）、equipment（装备）、gym（健身房）
运动精神与赛事：spirit（精神）、perseverance（坚持）、teamwork（团队合作）、competition（比赛）、match（赛事）、champion（冠军）、participate（参与）、challenge（挑战）
② 段首句、主题句、段尾句
段首句：
a)Doing sports regularly is of great benefit to our physical and mental health.
b)Among all sports, I like running best because it is simple and effective.
c)Our school will hold a sports meeting next month, and various exciting events are waiting for everyone to participate in.
d)If you want to keep fit but don't know which sport to choose, I have some good suggestions for you.
主题句：
a)Running can not only improve our cardiopulmonary function but also help us relieve the pressure from study and work.
b)Teamwork is the key to winning in collective sports such as basketball and football.
c)When doing sports, we must pay attention to safety to avoid unnecessary injuries.
d)The marathon not only tests people's physical endurance but also embodies the spirit of never giving up.
段尾句：
a)Let’s take part in sports actively and enjoy the happiness brought by movement.
b)I believe that as long as we persist in doing sports, we will become healthier and more energetic.
c)I hope all students can actively participate in the sports meeting and show their style on the field.
d)Choosing the right sport and adhering to it will make our life more colorful and healthy.

三、翻译句子

  1. 均衡的饮食和规律的运动是保持健康的关键。
    A balanced diet and regular exercise are the keys to maintaining good health.
  2. 中国的烹饪风格因地区而异，每种菜系都有其独特的特点。
    Chinese cuisines vary from region to region, and each style has its own unique characteristics.
  3. 在端午节，人们通常吃粽子、赛龙舟来纪念屈原。
    On the Dragon Boat Festival, people usually eat zongzi and hold dragon boat races to honor Qu Yuan.
  4. 为了庆祝新年，家家户户都打扫房屋、贴春联、拜访亲友。
    To celebrate the New Year, every family cleans the house, pastes Spring Festival couplets and visits relatives and friends.
  5. 这位运动员花了五年时间训练，最终在全国比赛中获得了冠军。
    The athlete spent five years training and finally won the championship in the national competition.
  6. 学校新的体育设施为学生提供了更多锻炼的机会，深受大家欢迎。
    The new sports facilities in the school provide students with more opportunities to exercise and are very popular among everyone.
  7. 加工食品虽然方便，但通常含有过多的盐和糖，不宜多吃。
    Although processed food is convenient, it usually contains too much salt and sugar and should not be eaten too much.
  8. 咀嚼食物时要慢一些，这样有助于消化，也能更好地享受食物的味道。
    Chew your food slowly, which helps digestion and allows you to better enjoy the taste of the food.
