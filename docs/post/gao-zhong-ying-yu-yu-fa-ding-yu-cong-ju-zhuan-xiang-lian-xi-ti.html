2．I will never forget such a beautiful village I spent my childhood with my grandparents.
3．There they met people from other parts of the country，_ had also volunteered to help.
4．There are many good websites ________you can check out the latest in the science world.

7．Over the years，I have met some interesting people. Two of the most interesting people were two elderly sisters，Joan and Bernice， way of talking about people inspired me.
8． an official document explained，tea is a fundamental cultural element to Chinese people’s daily life.
9．The reason ________the 12th lunar month is called layue has a lot to do with the custom of sacrifice.
10．Zhou is working with a group of highly educated and skilled young people，most of __________hold doctorate degrees.

  1. which 2. where 3. who 4. where 5. who/that 6. when 7. whose 8. As 9. why 10. Whom
