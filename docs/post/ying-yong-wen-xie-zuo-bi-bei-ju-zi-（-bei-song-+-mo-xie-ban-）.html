应用文写作必备句子（背诵+默写版）

背诵版

  1. I am writing this letter to apply for the exchange student program of your school.
    我写这封信是为了申请贵校的交换生项目。
  2. Thank you very much for your warm help when I visited the school last week.
    非常感谢你上周在我参观学校时给予的热情帮助。
  3. I am sorry to inform you that I cannot attend tomorrow’s meeting due to a sudden illness.
    很抱歉告知你，我因突发疾病无法参加明天的会议。
  4. I am enclosing my resume and academic transcript for your reference.
    随信附上我的个人简历和成绩单，供你参考。
  5. I would like to inquire about the summer opening hours of the school library.
    我想咨询一下关于学校图书馆暑期开放时间的问题。
  6. I would appreciate it if you could reply to my email as soon as possible.
    希望你能尽快回复我的邮件，我将不胜感激。
  7. We plan to organize a tree-planting activity next Saturday and sincerely invite you to participate.
    我们计划下周六组织一次植树活动，诚邀你参加。
  8. Please send the registration form to the designated email address by this Friday.
    请在本周五之前将报名表格发送至指定邮箱。
  9. This activity aims to improve students’ environmental awareness and practical abilities.
    本次活动旨在提高学生的环保意识和实践能力。
  10. If you have any questions about the activity arrangement, please feel free to contact me.
    如果你对活动安排有任何疑问，欢迎随时联系我。
  11. On behalf of the Student Union, I am sending you an invitation to attend the New Year’s Party.
    我代表学生会向你发出参加新年晚会的邀请。
  12. The party will be held in the school auditorium at 7 p.m. on December 30th.
    晚会将于12月30日晚7点在学校礼堂举行。
  13. To ensure the smooth progress of the party, we hope you can arrive 15 minutes in advance.
    为了确保晚会顺利进行，希望你能提前15分钟到场。
  14. We have prepared a variety of programs and gifts, and we are looking forward to your arrival.
    我们准备了丰富的节目和礼品，期待你的到来。
  15. Thank you for your consistent support and understanding for the work of the Student Union.
    感谢你一直以来对学生会工作的支持与理解。
  16. I am writing this email to recommend Li Ming, a student of our school, to participate in the internship program of your company.
    我写这封邮件是为了推荐我校学生李明参加贵公司的实习项目。
  17. Li Ming has excellent academic performance and possesses strong communication skills and team spirit.
    李明学习成绩优异，且具备较强的沟通能力和团队合作精神。
  18. I believe he can make contributions to your company during the internship.
    相信他能够在实习期间为贵公司做出贡献。
  19. If you need more information about Li Ming, please feel free to contact me.
    如果你需要更多关于李明的信息，欢迎与我联系。
  20. Thank you again for considering my recommendation.
    再次感谢你考虑我的推荐。
  21. We regret to inform you that the English speech contest originally scheduled for next Wednesday will be postponed for one week.
    很遗憾通知你，原定于下周三的英语演讲比赛将推迟一周举行。
  22. The reason for the postponement is that there is an important event arranged in the school auditorium temporarily.
    推迟原因是学校礼堂临时有重要活动安排。
  23. The new contest time is 2 p.m. next Wednesday, and the venue remains unchanged.
    新的比赛时间为下周三下午2点，地点不变。
  24. We apologize for the inconvenience caused to you.
    给你带来的不便，我们深表歉意。
  25. Please adjust your preparation plan in time and inform each other.
    请及时调整你的准备计划，并相互转告。
  26. I would like to take this opportunity to express my gratitude to you for your help last time.
    我想借这个机会向你表达我对上次帮助的谢意。
  27. Your advice helped me achieve excellent results in the English writing competition.
    你的建议让我在英语写作比赛中取得了优异成绩。
  28. If you have time, I would like to treat you to a meal to show my thanks.
    如果你有时间，我想请你吃顿饭以表感谢。
  29. I look forward to having the opportunity to communicate with you further.
    期待能有机会与你进一步交流。
  30. Thank you again for your generous help.
    再次感谢你的慷慨相助。
  31. This notice is intended to inform all students about the arrangement of the summer social practice activity.
    本通知旨在告知全体学生关于暑期社会实践活动的安排。
  32. The activity will last for one month from July 10th to August 10th.
    活动时间为7月10日至8月10日，为期一个月。
  33. Students can choose different practice projects according to their own interests.
    学生可根据自己的兴趣选择不同的实践项目。
  34. Please register with your head teacher before June 20th.
    请于6月20日前在班主任处报名。
  35. After the activity, students are required to submit a practice report.
    活动结束后，学生需提交一份实践报告。
  36. I am writing this letter to complain about the quality of the food in the school canteen.
    我写这封信是为了投诉学校食堂的饭菜质量问题。
  37. Recently, the food in the canteen not only has a price increase but also tastes worse.
    最近食堂的饭菜不仅价格上涨，而且口味变差。
  38. Some dishes also have hygiene problems, which affect students’ health.
    部分菜品还存在卫生问题，影响学生健康。
  39. We hope the school can solve these problems as soon as possible and improve the canteen conditions.
    希望学校能尽快解决这些问题，改善食堂条件。
  40. We look forward to the school’s reply and handling result.
    期待学校的回复和处理结果。
  41. You are welcome to join our English study group to improve our English level together.
    欢迎你加入我们的英语学习小组，共同提高英语水平。
  42. The group activity time is from 4 p.m. to 5 p.m. every Tuesday and Thursday.
    小组活动时间为每周二和周四下午4点至5点。
  43. The activity venue is the study room on the third floor of the school library.
    活动地点在学校图书馆三楼的自习室。
  44. We will carry out activities such as word recitation, oral dialogue and reading sharing.
    我们会开展单词背诵、口语对话和阅读分享等活动。
  45. If you are interested, you can go directly to participate during the activity time.
    如果你有兴趣，可直接在活动时间前往参加。
  46. I would like to consult you on how to apply for the school scholarship.
    我想向你咨询如何申请学校的奖学金。
  47. Could you tell me what conditions are required to apply for the scholarship?
    请问申请奖学金需要满足哪些条件？
  48. Before what time do the application materials need to be submitted?
    申请材料需要在什么时间之前提交？
  49. What is the selection process of the scholarship?
    奖学金的评选流程是怎样的？
  50. Thank you for providing me with relevant information.
    感谢你为我提供相关信息。

背诵版
51. To celebrate the 50th anniversary of the school’s founding, we will hold a series of activities.
为了庆祝学校建校50周年，我们将举办一系列活动。
52. The activities include alumni forum, campus open day and art performance, etc.
活动包括校友座谈会、校园开放日和文艺汇演等。
53. The campus open day will be held on October 1st, and parents and people from all walks of life are welcome to attend.
校园开放日将于10月1日举行，欢迎家长和社会各界人士参加。
54. We hope all teachers and students will actively participate in various activities and jointly create a festive atmosphere.
希望全体师生积极参与各项活动，共同营造喜庆氛围。
55. Let’s send our best wishes for the development of the school together.
让我们一起为学校的发展送上祝福。
56. I am sorry to disturb you, but I would like to ask you to help revise my English composition.
很抱歉打扰你，我想请你帮忙修改一下我的英语作文。
57. This composition is prepared for the English competition, and I hope to get your guidance.
这篇作文是为参加英语竞赛准备的，希望能得到你的指导。
58. If you have time, I can bring the composition to you tomorrow.
如果你有时间，我可以明天把作文带给你。
59. No matter what the revision result is, I am very grateful for your help.
无论修改结果如何，我都非常感谢你的帮助。
60. I look forward to your reply.
期待你的回复。
61. Our school will hold a campus job fair next Friday, and many well-known enterprises will be invited.
我校将于下周五举办校园招聘会，邀请了多家知名企业。
62. The job fair will be held in the school gymnasium from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m.
招聘会时间为上午9点至下午4点，地点在学校体育馆。
63. It is suggested that graduates prepare their resumes in advance and dress properly to attend.
建议毕业生提前准备好简历，穿着得体参加。
64. Enterprises will give on-site lectures and conduct preliminary interviews, which is a rare opportunity.
企业将现场进行宣讲和初步面试，机会难得。
65. We hope graduates can seize this opportunity and find their ideal jobs.
希望毕业生能抓住这次机会，找到理想的工作。
66. I am writing this email to confirm whether I have successfully registered for the summer camp of your school.
我写这封邮件是为了确认我是否成功报名了贵校的夏令营。
67. I submitted the registration materials last week, but I haven’t received the confirmation information yet.
我于上周提交了报名材料，但至今未收到确认信息。
68. My name is Zhang Hua, and my registration number is XM20240608.
我的姓名是张华，报名编号为XM20240608。
69. I hope you can help check and inform me of the result as soon as possible.
希望你能帮忙查询并尽快告知我结果。
70. Thank you for your cooperation.
感谢你的配合。
71. To improve students’ safety awareness, the school will organize a safety knowledge lecture.
为了提高学生的安全意识，学校将组织一次安全知识讲座。
72. The lecture will be held in the school lecture hall at 3 p.m. next Tuesday.
讲座将于下周二下午3点在学校报告厅举行。
73. Experts from the public security bureau are invited to give a lecture this time.
本次讲座邀请了公安局的专家进行讲解。
74. All students are required to attend and shall not be absent.
要求全体学生必须参加，不得缺席。
75. The monitor of each class is requested to count the number of students and keep records.
请各班班长负责清点人数，并做好记录。
76. On behalf of the class, I apply to the school for borrowing the multimedia classroom to hold a theme class meeting.
我代表班级向学校申请借用多媒体教室举办主题班会。
77. The theme of the class meeting is “Civilized Etiquette Accompanies Me”, and the time is scheduled for the 8th period on Friday afternoon.
班会主题为“文明礼仪伴我行”，时间定在本周五下午第8节课。
78. Borrowing the multimedia classroom is to better display relevant materials and videos.
借用多媒体教室是为了更好地展示相关资料和视频。
79. We will take good care of the classroom facilities and clean up the hygiene in time after the activity.
我们会爱护教室设施，活动结束后及时清理卫生。
80. We hope the school can approve our application.
希望学校能批准我们的申请。
81. Thank you very much for the birthday gift you sent me; I like it very much.
很感谢你寄来的生日礼物，我非常喜欢。
82. Your gift made me feel the deep friendship, and I am very moved.
你的礼物让我感受到了浓浓的友谊，我很感动。
83. When I have time, I will thank you in person.
等我有时间，一定当面谢谢你。
84. I also wish you happiness every day and all the best.
也祝你每天开心，一切顺利。
85. I look forward to our next meeting.
期待我们下次见面。
86. Our school plans to organize students to visit the History Museum to enrich their cultural knowledge.
我校计划组织学生前往历史博物馆参观，丰富学生的文化知识。
87. The visiting time is from 8 a.m. to 11 a.m. next Thursday, and the gathering place is at the school gate.
参观时间为下周四上午8点至11点，集合地点在学校门口。
88. Students need to bring their own drinking water and notebooks to record the visiting content.
学生需自带饮用水和笔记本，便于记录参观内容。
89. During the visit, students should abide by the museum regulations, keep quiet and not touch the exhibits at will.
参观期间要遵守博物馆规定，保持安静，不随意触摸展品。
90. We hope students can gain something from this visit.
希望学生能通过这次参观有所收获。
91. I am writing this letter to reflect the problem of traffic congestion around the school.
我写这封信是为了反映学校周边交通拥堵的问题。
92. During the morning and afternoon rush hours every day, there are many vehicles at the school gate, which poses a safety hazard.
每天上下学时段，学校门口车辆众多，存在安全隐患。
93. It is suggested that the school communicate with the traffic department to increase traffic guidance personnel.
建议学校与交通部门沟通，增加交通疏导人员。
94. We also hope that parents will try their best to choose green travel methods to reduce traffic congestion.
也希望家长尽量选择绿色出行方式，减少车辆拥堵。
95. We look forward to the effective solution of these problems to ensure students’ safety.
期待这些问题能得到有效解决，保障学生安全。
96. Our club plans to hold a calligraphy competition and is now collecting works from all students in the school.
我们社团计划举办一场书法比赛，现向全校学生征集作品。
97. The theme of the works is not limited, and the calligraphy styles can be regular script, running script or cursive script.
作品主题不限，书法字体可选择楷书、行书或草书。
98. The deadline for submitting works is 6 p.m. next Sunday, and the submission place is the club office.
作品提交截止时间为下周日下午6点，提交地点在社团办公室。
99. The competition will select the first, second and third prizes, and the winning works will be exhibited on campus.
比赛将评选出一、二、三等奖，获奖作品将在校园内展出。
100. Students who are interested are welcome to actively participate and show their calligraphy talents.
欢迎有兴趣的学生积极参与，展示自己的书法才华。
