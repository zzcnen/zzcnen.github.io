高中英语语法名词性从句专项练习题

一、基础达标练
1．He was not looking for others’ smiles，or worrying about _________someone was watching or not.
2．Helen McCarthy，an author and expert on Japanese Cartoons，explained _______the character is so popular. “Because Hello Kitty is all about happiness，friendship and fun.”
3．My first task was to decide where to go and ________to get there.
4．Students can choose ___________they want to do and register online.
5．Mr White is opposed to repairing the old building，and that’s ________I don’t agree.
6．The problem is _______one less hour of sleep is not equal to an extra hour of achievement.
7．Twenty-five years later the traveler returned to the same place. ________he saw amazed him. He could not believe his own eyes.
8．It was not clear _________the water in the reservoir would rise again，and the exposed walls of large buildings in the old city would disappear.
9．The outputs are amazingly varied depending on _______is writing the orders.

参考答案：

  1. whether 2. why 3. how 4. what/whatever 5. where 6. that 7. What 8. when 9. who

四、语法链接写作
1．what引导的名词性从句最显眼
典例　她感到很有成就感，并开始思考是什么帮助她完成这次跑步。
She felt very accomplished and began to reflect whathad helped her to finish this run.
运用　在持续了似乎有一个世纪之久的激烈讨论之后，Gunter放下手机，发动了汽车。
After a heated discussion that lasted for_______________，Gunter put his phone down and started the car.
2．同位语从句放在开头实力开篇
典例　我相信这样的活动对于促进跨文化理解和欣赏至关重要，我很高兴有机会分享自己的技能和经验，作为这项努力的一部分。
I hold the belief that such events are crucial for promoting cross-cultural understanding and appreciation，and I am thrilled to have the opportunity to share my own skills and experience as part of this effort.
运用　没有人能否认在线学习正变得越来越流行这一事实。

3．表语从句之前的连词要考虑周全
典例　我们必须采取措施来保护海洋的原因是它的污染正变得越来越严重。
The reason why we must take measures to protect the ocean is that its pollution is becoming more and more serious.
运用　很抱歉，我不能按计划和你出去。原因是我弟弟前几天摔断了腿，我必须照顾他。

4．That/This is because/why...灵活简练
典例　他们更容易沉迷于网上聊天或玩游戏，那是因为他们缺乏自律。
They’re more easily addicted to chatting online or playing games. That’s because they lack self-discipline.
运用　(2021·浙江6月)这个展览非常精彩，展示了中国文化的魅力。这就是为什么我想推荐我的同学们也来参加这个艺术展。

5．注意名词性从句与虚拟语气的结合
典例　相反，我建议我们应该实行一个让学生能根据自己的语言能力和个人喜好选择自己同伴的制度。
Instead，I suggest we shouldimplement a system where students can choose their own partners based on their language abilities and personal preferences.
运用　我建议你选择听说课程，因为这门课程完全是用中文教授的，这会对你有很大的好处。

参考答案：

  1. what seemed like a century 2. No one can deny the fact that online learning is becoming more and more popular 3. I’m sorry that I can’t go out with you as planned. The reason is that my younger brother broke his leg the other day and I must look after him.4. The exhibition is fantastic and shows the charm of Chinese culture. This is why I would like to recommend my fellow students to attend this art show as well.5.I recommend that you (should) choose the Listening and Speaking course，because the course is entirely taught in Chinese，which will benefit you a lot.

五、语篇写作
假定你是校广播站英语节目“Talk and Talk”的负责人李华，请给外教Caroline写封信邀请她做一次访谈。内容包括：
1.节目介绍；
2.访谈的时间和话题。

Dear Caroline，
I am writing the letter to invite you to an interview held by “Talk and Talk”，1.________________________ (这是我们学校广播站的一个英语节目)．
As you know，our programme starts at 10 a．m. and ends at 11 a．m. every Tuesday. Besides，the topic is about 2.______________ (我们怎样才能提高英语口语)．You can give us some suggestions about it. It is certain 3._______________________________ (所有的学生都将从你的访谈中受益良多)．
I would appreciate it 4.____________________________ (如果你能考虑一下我的邀请)．Looking forward to your early reply.
Yours sincerely，
Li Hua

参考答案：

  1. which is an English programme in our school broadcast station;2. how we can improve our spoken English;3. that all students will benefit much from your interview;4. if you could take my invitation into account
