一、核心语言点汇总
（一）marriage 相关
marriage n. 婚姻
marry v. 结婚
married adj. 已婚的 – single adj. 单身的 – engaged adj. 订婚的 – divorced adj. 离婚的
常用搭配：
be married to sb 与某人已结婚
get married to sb 与某人结婚
例句：
He married Bea in 1925.
She married at a young age.
She is married to an Australian. 她和一个澳大利亚人结婚。
They have been married for 15 years. 他们已经结婚 15 年了。

（二）sort 相关
sort n. 种类 vt. 将…… 分类；整理
常用搭配：
sort of = kind of 有点
all sorts of 各种各样的
sort out 挑出；整理
sort into 整理；分类
例句：
He spent an afternoon sorting out his stamps.
I sort of like him, but I don’t know why.
What sort of music do you like?
Let’s sort all the clothes into piles.

（三）typical 相关
typical adj. 典型的；有代表性的
typically adv. 通常；典型地；一向如此
常用搭配：
be typical of ... 典型的……；特有的……
It is typical of sb. to do sth. 某人一向做某事；做某事是某人的特点
例句：
It was typical of her to be late.
His childhood lacked the harmony that was typical of a happy family.
The workers are typically middle-aged men.

（四）suffer 相关
suffer v.
suffering n. (肉体或精神上的) 痛苦，苦难，折磨
sufferer n. 患者﹔受害者﹐受苦者
用法：
suffer vt.：接「抽象的、负面的、无生命的」名词，指「遭受 / 蒙受（损失、痛苦、失败等）」，强调被动承受，搭配词：pain、loss、damage、harm 等
例句：He suffered a great loss in the fire. 他在火灾中蒙受了巨大损失。
suffer vi.：后面不加宾语，意思是「受苦、受难、遭殃」，侧重主观感受
例句：The poor people suffered a lot in the past. 穷人在过去吃了很多苦。
suffer from 用法：
接「具体的疾病名词」→ 表示「患… 病，受… 病痛折磨」
搭配：suffer from a cold/headache/fever/cancer 等
例句：She suffers from a bad back all the time. 她一直受背痛的折磨。
接「长期的、持续性的问题 / 困扰」→ 表示「受… 问题困扰」
搭配：suffer from poverty/stress/loneliness 等
例句：Many students suffer from stress before exams. 很多学生考前都承受着压力。
接「某种不利的状况 / 缺点」→ 表示「因…… 而吃亏 / 受影响」
例句：This plant suffers from lack of sunlight. 这株植物因为缺光长势不好。
语法点：suffer from 不能用「被动语态」
练习：
She suffered from a bad cold last week.（上周她得了重感冒 → 疾病，用 suffer from）
His family suffered a great loss in the earthquake.（他家在地震中损失惨重 → 损失，用 suffer）
Many animals suffer hunger in winter.（很多动物冬天挨饿 → 饥饿，抽象损失，用 suffer）
He has suffered from depression for many years.（他患抑郁症多年 → 长期疾病，用 suffer from）

（五）gather 相关
gather v. 聚集；集合；搜集；采集
gathering n. 聚会；集会
gatherer n. 收集者，采集者
常用搭配：
gather together 聚集起来；合拢
gather round /around 聚集；围着
例句：
Gather round, everyone, so that you can see the screen.
A crowd gathered to watch the fight.
They were an ancient people who lived by hunting and gathering.

（六）bite 相关
bite n. v. 咬（过去式：bit；过去分词：bitten）
常用搭配：have/take a bite of sth 咬一口
例句：
The dog bit him and made his hand bleed.
She was bitten by the family dog.
He took several bites of the apple.

（七）poison 相关
poison n. 毒素﹔毒物﹔毒药 v. 毒害；下毒
poisonous adj. 有毒的
谚语：One man’s meat is another man’s poison. 一人手中宝，他人脚下草。
例句：
Some mushrooms contain a deadly poison.
Poisoning the rats is expensive and harmful to the environment.
The plant is poisonous to humans and animals.

（八）symbol 相关
symbol n. 象征（~of）；符号（~for）；代表（~of）
symbolic adj. 象征的；象征性的
symbolise / symbolize vt. 象征；代表
例句：
The dove is a symbol of peace.
Fe is the chemical symbol for iron.
The ceremony has a deep symbolic meaning.
Crime often symbolizes a wider social problem.

（九）opinion 相关
opinion n. 意见，看法，主张
常用搭配：
hold /have an opinion 持有某种观点
voice one's opinion 发表意见
in one’s opinion 依某人看来 = in one’s view = as far as one is concerned

（十）function 相关
function n. 功能；职责；作用；函数 v. 起作用；运转
functional adj. 功能的；实用的；起作用的
例句：
In your new job you will perform a variety of functions.
The nervous system regulates our bodily functions.
Flights in and out of Taipei are functioning normally again.
The sofa also functions as a bed.
The building's design is not only functional but also beautiful.

（十一）download 与 upload
download v. 下载
upload v. 上传
例句：
The series is now available to download.
She uploaded the pictures from her digital camera to her computer.

（十二）related 相关
related adj. 相关的；有联系的 – irrelated adj. 不相干的
relevant adj. 有关的 – irrelevant adj. 无关的
relation / relationship n. 关系；联系
常用搭配：
be related to ... 和…… 相关
be connected with
be in connection with
be linked to / with
have something / nothing to do with sth
例句：Education levels are strongly /closely/directly related to income.

（十三）diet 相关
diet n. 饮食 v. 节食
常用搭配：
be on a diet 在节食
go on a diet 开始节食
例句：
Lyn always seems to be on a diet. 琳似乎总在节食。
Not all diets are good for you. 并非所有饮食都适合你。
I really ought to go on a diet. 我真该节食了。

（十四）addict 相关
addict n. 吸毒成瘾的人；对… 上瘾的人
addiction n. 瘾；嗜好；入迷
addictive adj. (尤指药物) 使人上瘾的；使人入迷的（形容物）
addicted adj. 成瘾的；上瘾的（形容人）
常用搭配：be/get addicted to ... 对…… 上瘾；对…… 着迷（to 为介词）
例句：
I used to be a video game addict — I almost spent all my free time playing games.
It started as a hobby, but it got so addictive I had to keep on doing it.
He admitted he was addicted to cocaine.
He is now fighting his addiction to alcohol.

（十五）custom 相关词汇辨析
词汇	词性	含义
custom	n.	风俗；习俗（特定社会 / 地方的传统做法）
customer	n.	顾客
customs	n.	海关
costume	n.	服饰；戏服
consume	v.	吃；喝；消费；消耗
consumer	n.	消费者
tradition	n.	传统；传统的信仰（或行事方式）（长期存在的群体信念）
convention	n.	习俗﹔惯例﹐常规（社会普遍认可的行为态度）
例句：
The bride's parents are by tradition expected to pay for the wedding.
They were unfamiliar with local customs and culture.
As a matter of convention, the oldest members speak first.

（十六）in case 相关短语
核心短语：
in case 以防万一；如果（可引导条件 / 目的状语从句，单独使用；接名词用 in case of）
in case of 如果；假使
in any case 在任何情况下；不管怎样
in this case 在这种情况下
in that case 既然如此
in one’s case 就某人而言；在… 的案例中
in no case 决不（置于句首，句中部分倒装）
例句：
Please remind me of the meeting again tomorrow in case I forget. 请明天再提醒我一次开会事宜，免得我忘了。
Please inform me in advance in case of any change. 如有什么变化，请提前通知我。
练习：
You may think of giving up study. In this case, just think it over before making the decision.
—He didn’t want to talk to Sally. —In that case, why did he agree to meet her?
I don’t see why I couldn’t do it. In any case, I’m going to try.
In no case should you leave your post.

（十七）trick 相关
trick n. 花招；骗局；诡计；戏法；诀窍；技巧 v. 欺骗
tricky adj. 狡猾的，机警的；棘手的，难对付的
固定表达：trick or treat（万圣节用语：是请吃糖，还是想遭殃）
例句：
The trick is to pick the animal up by the back of its neck.
I thought he was playing a trick on me.（捉弄）
He does this great trick with a hat and two rabbits.
I can get you tickets for the show but it’ll be tricky.

（十八）handle 相关
handle n. 把手 v. 处理；应对（= deal with /cope with）
例句：
The headmaster handled the situation very well.
Some customers are quite difficult to handle.
She turned the handle and opened the door.

（十九）differ 相关
differ v. 不同，有区别；有分歧
difference n. 不同 – different adj. 不同的 – differently adv. 有差别地
常用搭配：
differ from ... 与… 不同
differ in ... 在…… 方面不同
differ with sb. 与某人意见不同
differ (with) sb. on/about/over sth. (与某人) 在某事上意见不同
例句：
People differ from one another in their ability to handle stress.
They differed with each other on / over / about religious matters.

（二十）slightly 相关
slightly adv. 稍微
slight adj. 轻微的；略微的
例句：
The price has increased slightly.
Doctors say he has made a slight improvement.

（二十一）incredibly 相关
incredibly adv. 非常；极其；难以置信地（= unbelievably）
incredible adj. 极好的；极大的；难以置信的
例句：
Being tall can make you feel incredibly self-confident.
The knife had pierced his heart, but incredibly he was still alive.
It’s incredible how much Tom has changed since he met Sally.
He seemed to spend an incredible amount of money.

（二十二）recommend 相关
recommend v. 推荐；介绍；建议
recommendation n. 提议，推荐，推荐信
常用搭配：
recommend sb. to do sth. 建议某人做……
recommend sth. to sb. = recommend sb. sth. 向某人推荐某事 / 某物
recommend doing sth. 建议做某事
recommend that sb. (should) do ... 建议某人做某事
It's recommend that... (should) do ... 建议……
同类动词（虚拟语气）：一坚持（insist）、二命令（command, order）、四建议（suggest, advise, propose, recommend）、六要求（ask, require, request, demand, urge, desire）
练习：
The doctor recommended me to take a long rest.
If you go to Beijing, I recommend visiting the Summer Palace first.
It is strongly recommended that the machines (should) be checked every year.

（二十三）attack 相关
attack v. 侵袭；袭击 n. 攻击；抨击；（疾病）发作
attacker n. 袭击者
常用搭配：
attack sb. with sth. 用某物攻击某人
launch /make an attack on/upon... 对… 发动进攻
under attack 受到攻击
a heart attack 心脏病发作
近形词：attract vt. 吸引
例句：
Of course, they should not feed them in a close distance because the wild animals may attack people.（江西卷）
When an animal is under attack, it can run away or fight back. 动物遭到攻击时，它会逃跑或反击。
The enemy made an attack on/upon the city last night. 昨晚敌人对这座城市进行了袭击。
练习：Bob died of a heart attack brought on by his lifestyle. 鲍勃死于由他的生活方式引起的心脏病发作。

（二十四）belong to 相关
belong to 属于
belongings n.[pl.] 所有物；财产，财物
a sense of belonging 归属感
语法点：belong to 不用于进行时态，无被动语态；后接人称代词用宾格
例句：
The tourists lost all their belongings in the hotel fire.
The house belongs to Tom, but he doesn’t live here.
—Have you seen a dictionary belonging to our school library? —Yes, it is over there and Mary is using it.
He has no sense of belonging here.
练习（一句多译）：
As is known to us, China is a country belonging to developing countries.
It is known to us that China is a country which belongs to developing countries.（众所周知，中国属于发展中国家。）

（二十五）end up 相关
核心搭配：
end up doing 以做某事而告终
end up with ... 以… 结束（后接具体事物名词）
end up in ... 以… 结束（后接抽象概念名词）
end up ＋ adj. 以…(状况) 而告终
end up (as) + n. 最终成为 / 变成…
bring ... to an end 使… 结束 = put/bring an end to ... 结束…
come to an end 结束
in the end 最后，终于
at the end of 在… 尽头 / 末尾 / 结束时
例句：
You’ll soon end up in debt if you keep on spending money that way. 如果你继续那样花钱，你很快就会负债的。
He began his life as a poor man and ended up as a millionaire. 他出身贫寒，最终成了百万富翁。
At the dinner we usually begin with soup and end up with fruit. 晚宴上，我们通常最先喝汤，最后吃水果。

（二十六）bring up 相关

（二十七）construction 相关
construction n.
construct vt. 建造，建筑
reconstruct vt. 重建；重现
constructive adj. 建设性的；积极的
常用搭配：under construction 在建造中
例句：

They are planning to construct a new road bridge across the river. 有计划要建造一条新的跨河公路桥。
The hotel is currently under construction. 这家宾馆目前正在建造中。

（二十八）satisfy 相关
satisfy vt. （使）满足
satisfied adj. 感到满意的（形容人）
satisfaction n. 满足；满意；欣慰
satisfactory adj. 令人满意的（指事物达到要求）
satisfying adj. 令人满意的（指事物带来快乐感）
常用搭配：
be/feel satisfied with 对… 感到满意 = be pleased /content with
to one’s satisfaction 令某人满意的是
例句：
It’s satisfying to play a game really well. 一种游戏玩得特别好是一桩惬意的事。
The young man found it hard to satisfy his boss, so he left. 这个年轻人发现很难让老板满意，于是离开了。
Much to my satisfaction, my boss agreed to my suggestion. 使我非常满意的是老板同意了我的建议。
练习：
He is a modest man and never feels satisfied with his present achievements.
From the satisfied smile on her face, we can see the performance was satisfying.

（二十九）convenient 相关
convenient adj. 方便的，便利的
convenience n. 方便，便利
inconvenient adj. 不方便的
常用搭配：
at one's convenience 在某人方便的时候
It is convenient (for sb.) to do sth. (某人) 做某事很方便
sth. be convenient to/for sb. 对某人来说… 是方便的
语法点：convenient 作表语时，不可用人作主语，要用物或形式主语 it
例句：
Subways are fast and convenient, but rush hours can be terrible. 地铁方便快捷，但在交通高峰期，情况可能会很糟糕。
Our house is very convenient to/for schools and stores. 我们的房子离学校和商店很近。
We hope that you will accept our invitation if it is convenient for you. 如果方便的话，我们希望您能接受我们的邀请。
Would it be convenient for you to pick me up at four o’clock and take me to the airport?（山东卷）你方便 4 点来接我并送我去机场吗？
We should be grateful if you would reply at your earliest convenience. 如蒙速复﹐不胜感激。
练习：The Internet makes it more convenient for us to get in touch with each other. 互联网使我们相互联系更方便了。

（三十）identify 相关

例句：

（三十三）adapt 相关
adapt v. (使) 适应；（使）适合

常用搭配：

例句：

When you go to a new country, you must adapt yourself to new customs. 当你到一个新的国家，你必须使自己适应新的风俗习惯。

（三十四）pick up 相关含义
核心含义：
